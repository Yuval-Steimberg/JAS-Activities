## 💵 Discount Structure

### Standard Discount
- **Discount Rate:** 20%
- **Applied to:** Subtotal before final calculation

### Example Calculation

**Scenario:** 15 participants
- Tour: ₪1,000 (flat rate)
- Workshop: ₪2,400 × 15 = ₪36,000
- Gift: ₪60 × 15 = ₪900
- Catering: ₪40 × 15 = ₪600

**Calculation:**
```
Subtotal:        ₪38,500
Discount (20%):  -₪7,700
───────────────────────
Final Total:     ₪30,800
```
